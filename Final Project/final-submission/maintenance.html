Maintenance

It is important to keep your dog's health and happiness a top priority.

Exercise

Dogs need exercise in order to burn calories, stay healthy, and stimulate thier minds. Exercise needs vary based on breed, sex, age, and health level. Exercise also prevents boredom which can lead to destructive behaviors.

Grooming

Keep your dog clean and reduce shedding with frequent brushing. During warm weather check for fleas and ticks daily. Most dogs only need to be bathed a few times a year.

  • Important Grooming Tools:
  • Blow Dryer
  • Bristle Brush
  • Comb
  • Conditioning Spray
  • Ear Cleaning Solution
  • Nail Clippers
  • Shampoo and Conditioner
  • Toothbrush and Dog Toothpaste
  • Towels

Handling

To carry a puppy or small dog, place your hand under the dog's chest with your forearm or other hand supporting the hind legs and rump. Do not grab or lift your puppy or small dog by the forelegs, tail, or back of neck.

Medications and Vaccinations

Never give your dog medication that has not been prescribed by a veterinarian. Vaccinations help prepare the body's immune system to fight disease. Your dog may benefit from receiving a number of vaccinations.
