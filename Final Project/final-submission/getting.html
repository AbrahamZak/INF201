Getting a Dog

If you've decided to get a dog, there are some things that need to be considered.

Are you sure you're ready?

  • Can You Commit? This is a very important decision and you will need to know if you are 100% ready to take care of your new friend.
  • Will Your Pet Fit Your Lifestyle? Remember that you can't simply choose your new friend based on its cuteness. You'll also have to check if the breed you want will be able to live with your lifestyle and be healthy and happy.
  • Will you be able to train your dog? It is very important to start early on training your dog.

Things you will need to get started:

  • Collar and leash
  • Dog bed
  • Food and water bowls
  • Food and treats
  • Grooming supplies
  • Toys

Adopting

If you plan to adopt, here's some things you should consider.

  • You can save a life
  • Not too expensive
  • Adopting an older dog means he or she is probably already housebroken.

Breeders

If you plan to go through a breeder, here's some things you should consider.

  • You can see the parents and genetics
  • Expensive
  • Adopting as a puppy means you can train and teach your dog from the very start
